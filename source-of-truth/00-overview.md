# Study Buddy Pro Source Of Truth

Status: decided

Study Buddy Pro is a local-first desktop tutor for students. It is forked from Thuki, but the canonical product is no longer a generic floating secretary. The app must help a learner understand material step by step through local AI, screenshots/images, guided questions, quizzes, vocabulary mastery, and local text-to-speech.

The app must run as a separate product from an installed Thuki app. It uses its own bundle identifier, data directory, configuration, database, and visible product name.

Core promise:

- The student can show Study Buddy Pro what they are studying.
- The app explains in small pieces instead of dumping a full generic answer.
- The app checks understanding with questions and adapts from mistakes.
- The app can speak during guided study sessions.
- Progress and learner patterns stay local by default.
