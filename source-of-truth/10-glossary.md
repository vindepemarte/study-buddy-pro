# Glossary

- Study Mode: guided tutoring mode with small steps, spoken prompts, checks, and persisted learning events.
- Mastery: local evidence that the student can explain or apply a concept without shallow repetition.
- Vocabulary loop: a teaching loop where the student writes original sentences for each definition until mastery.
- Supertonic sidecar: the bundled local HTTP service that turns tutor text into speech.
- Search box: local Docker SearXNG and reader services used by `/search`.
